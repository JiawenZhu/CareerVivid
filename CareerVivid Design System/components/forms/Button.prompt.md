CareerVivid button — short verb-phrase labels in sentence case.

```jsx
<Button icon="wand-2">Tailor resume</Button>
<Button variant="soft" icon="mic">Practice</Button>
<Button variant="neutral" size="sm">Cancel</Button>
```

Variants: primary (#625bd5 → #514ac5 hover), soft (soft-purple bg + ring — interview-guide CTAs, secondary actions), neutral (white + gray border), danger (rose tint, sparse destructive only). Sizes sm/md/lg = 32/36/44px.
