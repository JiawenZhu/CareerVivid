# 🚀 Announcing: Retro Windows Portfolio & AI-Native MCP Capabilities!

We've just shipped a major update to CareerVivid that brings a blast from the past and a leap into the future of personal branding.

## 📺 The Retro Windows Portfolio
Our newest portfolio template, `dev_terminal`, is finally live! Inspired by the classic Windows 95/98 aesthetic, it features:
- **Interactive Windows**: Draggable and resizable project windows.
- **Boot Sequence**: A nostalgic terminal boot-up animation.
- **Productivity-First**: A clean, windowed layout for your tech stack, timeline, and case studies.

## 🤖 AI-Native Portfolio Management (MCP)
Alongside the new template, we're launching the `careervivid-mcp-server`. This allows AI agents (like Claude or Cursor) to:
- **Live-Update Your Work**: Add projects, update your bio, or refine your tech stack directly from your IDE.
- **Automatic Sync**: Changes made via the MCP server automatically sync to your public portfolio and even append relevant project highlights to your active Resume.

## 🛠️ CLI v1.2.0
The `careervivid` CLI has been bumped to **v1.2.0**, featuring improved stability and full support for the new portfolio templates.

### How to get started:
1. Update your CLI: `npm install -g careervivid`
2. Initialize your retro portfolio: `cv portfolio init --template dev_terminal`
3. Push some projects: `cv portfolio add-project "My Cool App" --desc "..."`

Check out a live demo of the Retro Portfolio here: [Live Demo](https://careervivid.app/portfolio/RGYgVxggqOWCq65lkBmL)

Happy building! 🚀
