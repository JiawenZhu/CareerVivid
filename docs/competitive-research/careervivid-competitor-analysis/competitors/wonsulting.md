# Wonsulting — Competitor Profile

> **Threat Level: 🟢 LOW**
> **Category fit:** Professional Services Access
> **XPRIZE risk:** 500K+ users, but human-coaching-first model is not AI-native

---

## Overview
Wonsulting is a career coaching company founded by two Harvard graduates who went viral on LinkedIn and TikTok with job search advice. They've built a platform combining human career coaching with some AI tools (ResumAI). Revenue comes from coaching packages ($97–$497) and a growing digital product line.

## Key Capabilities
| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Human Career Coaching | 1:1 sessions with career coaches |
| Group Coaching Programs | Cohort-based job search programs |
| ResumAI | AI resume builder (GPT-based) |
| LinkedInAI | LinkedIn optimization tool |
| Job Search Academy | Video course library |
| Community | Discord/Slack community for job seekers |

## Business Model & Revenue
| Product | Price | |
|---|---|---|
| Resume Review | $97 | One-time |
| Job Hunting Program | $297–$497 | Cohort-based |
| ResumAI (monthly) | $9.99–$19.99/mo | SaaS |
| LinkedIn Review | $149 | One-time |

**Revenue estimate:** Strong high-ticket revenue from coaching packages. 500K+ social following converts to coaching sales. SaaS revenue likely smaller.

## Weaknesses vs CareerVivid
- Human coaching doesn't scale — not AI-native
- GPT-based AI tools, not Gemini
- No autonomous apply
- No job tracker / Kanban
- No CLI tool, no MCP server
- No B2B employer portal
- No Chrome extension
- Business model depends on human coaches, not AI operations

## XPRIZE Assessment
Low threat for XPRIZE. Their business is fundamentally human services with AI add-ons. XPRIZE explicitly requires AI to govern key business decisions — a human coach business cannot score well on AI-Native Operations. Their revenue from coaching packages is real but comes from the human side, not the AI side.

## What to Steal
- Their social-first growth strategy (LinkedIn/TikTok viral content) generated 500K followers — CareerVivid should build a content playbook with "job search tip" videos using the product itself as a demo
- The cohort/community model creates accountability and higher completion rates — CareerVivid could offer "job search accountability groups" powered by AI progress tracking
