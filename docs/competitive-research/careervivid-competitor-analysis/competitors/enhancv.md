# Enhancv — Competitor Profile

> **Threat Level: 🟡 MEDIUM**
> **Category fit:** Professional Services Access
> **XPRIZE risk:** 1M+ users, $24.99/mo, premium resume design focus + ChatGPT integration

---

## Overview
Enhancv targets professionals who want visually distinctive, well-designed resumes rather than just ATS-optimized ones. Their templates are premium-looking, and they've integrated ChatGPT for content generation. With 1M+ users and $24.99/month pricing, they occupy the "premium resume design" niche. Their audience tends to be mid-career professionals and designers rather than entry-level job seekers.

## Key Capabilities
| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Resume Templates | 50+ visually distinctive templates |
| AI Content Writer | ChatGPT-powered section writing |
| Impact Metrics | Quantified achievement suggestions |
| Resume Sections | Custom sections (interests, volunteering, etc.) |
| ATS Checker | Basic keyword check |
| Cover Letter Builder | AI-generated, template-matched |
| Resume Statistics | Views, downloads tracking if shared |
| Resume Review Service | Human expert review add-on ($49) |

## Business Model & Revenue
| Tier | Price | Features |
|---|---|---|
| Free | $0 | Limited templates, watermarked |
| Monthly | $24.99/mo | Full access, unlimited, no watermark |
| Annual | $13/mo equiv. | All features |

**Revenue estimate:** 1M+ users; conservative 2% paid = 20,000 × $19 avg = ~$380K MRR.

## Tech Stack
- **AI:** ChatGPT (OpenAI) — **NOT Gemini**, XPRIZE weakness
- **Frontend:** React
- **Design System:** Custom resume rendering engine
- **Human Review Add-on:** $49 per review (interesting hybrid AI + human model)

## Weaknesses vs CareerVivid
- ChatGPT/OpenAI, not Gemini
- No job tracker
- No voice interview coaching
- No autonomous apply
- No CLI tool
- No MCP server
- No B2B employer portal
- No Chrome extension for job sites
- Focused on aesthetics over workflow — limited repeat-use value after resume is created
- Human review is inconsistent and doesn't scale well

## XPRIZE Assessment
Enhancv will struggle with AI-Native Operations scoring — their core value is template design, and AI is an add-on for content. Judges won't find AI governing business decisions here. Their human review service is the opposite of AI-native.

## What to Steal
- Human expert review add-on ($49/session) is an interesting upsell that CareerVivid's interview coaching model could expand: offer human career coach review sessions as a premium tier
- "Quantified impact" prompts ("instead of 'managed projects,' say 'managed 3 projects worth $2M'") — this should be a default Gemini suggestion in CareerVivid's resume builder
- Design/template quality matters for user pride in the output — invest in template aesthetics even if AI generates content
