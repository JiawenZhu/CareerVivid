# Rezi.ai — Competitor Profile

> **Threat Level: 🟡 MEDIUM**
> **Category fit:** Professional Services Access
> **XPRIZE risk:** 4.3M+ users, $29/mo, strong resume brand but limited AI depth

---

## Overview
Rezi is one of the most recognized AI resume builders, with 4.3M+ users and a clean template-first approach. Founded in 2015, Rezi predates the LLM era and has layered GPT-based AI features on top of their template business. They have significant brand recognition and user base, but their core technology is template-rendering with AI as an add-on, not an AI-native architecture.

## Key Capabilities
| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Resume Templates | 100+ ATS-friendly templates |
| AI Resume Writer | GPT-powered content generation |
| AI Bullet Generator | Generates experience bullets from job title |
| ATS Resume Checker | Keyword and format analysis |
| AI Cover Letter | Per-job cover letter generation |
| Resume Score | 0–100 score per template compliance |

## Business Model & Revenue
| Tier | Price | Features |
|---|---|---|
| Free | $0 | 1 resume, watermarked PDF |
| Pro | $29/mo or $99/yr | Unlimited, no watermark, AI features |

**Revenue estimate:** 4.3M users, even 0.5% paid = 21,500 × $29 = ~$623K MRR. However, many users are one-time resume downloads — low recurring retention.

## Tech Stack
- **AI:** GPT-3/GPT-4 (OpenAI) — **NOT Gemini**, XPRIZE weakness
- **Core:** Template rendering engine (LaTeX / React PDF)
- **Frontend:** React
- **No Chrome extension, no job tracker, no voice coaching**

## Weaknesses vs CareerVivid
- GPT-based, not Gemini
- No job tracker or application management
- No voice interview coaching
- No autonomous apply
- No CLI tool or MCP server
- No B2B employer portal
- No Chrome extension
- No Vertex AI or Firebase
- Template-first design — AI is a bolt-on, not the architecture
- High churn: users pay once to download a resume, then cancel

## XPRIZE Assessment
Rezi has strong user counts for documentation but will struggle on "AI-Native Operations." Their product is a document generator with AI add-ons — not a platform where AI governs business decisions. Judges will see through the user count to the underlying architecture.

## What to Steal
- The 100+ template library creates perceived value and SEO traffic — CareerVivid should have 20–30 curated templates even if AI generates the content
- The "Resume Score 0–100" number is sticky — users refresh and try to improve their score (gamification loop)
