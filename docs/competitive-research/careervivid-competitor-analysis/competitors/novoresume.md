# Novoresume — Competitor Profile

> **Threat Level: 🟢 LOW**
> **Category fit:** Professional Services Access
> **XPRIZE risk:** 4M+ users, $14.99–$19.99/mo, but minimal AI depth

---

## Overview
Novoresume is a resume builder focused on design quality and ease of use. With 4M+ registered users and a $14.99–$19.99/month price point, they're accessible and well-known. However, their AI integration is minimal — the product is primarily a drag-and-drop resume editor with some AI suggestions. Founded in 2014, they predate the AI era.

## Key Capabilities
| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Resume Builder | Drag-and-drop, 20+ templates |
| AI Content Suggestions | Basic GPT suggestions for sections |
| Cover Letter Builder | Template-based |
| ATS Optimization | Basic format compliance check |
| Online Resume | Hosted resume link |
| Career Blog | SEO-driven content (large archive) |

## Business Model & Revenue
| Tier | Price | Features |
|---|---|---|
| Free | $0 | 1 resume, Novoresume branding |
| Pro | $14.99/mo or $107.88/yr | Unlimited, no branding |

**Revenue estimate:** 4M users; 1% conversion = 40,000 × $15 = $600K MRR. However, significant portion is one-time downloaders. Lower recurring retention.

## Tech Stack
- **AI:** Minimal — mostly rule-based suggestions with some GPT
- **Core:** Template rendering, PDF export
- **No job tracker, no Chrome extension, no job search integration**

## Weaknesses vs CareerVivid
- Minimal AI — mostly a template tool
- No Gemini or Google Cloud
- No job tracking or application management
- No voice interview coaching
- No autonomous apply
- No CLI, no MCP server, no B2B employer portal
- High churn: users create one resume and leave
- Career blog is their main SEO moat, not product excellence

## XPRIZE Assessment
Very low XPRIZE threat. Their product architecture is pre-AI, and their user count is inflated by one-time users. They're unlikely to enter the competition, and if they do, they'll score poorly on AI-Native Operations and Business Viability (high churn, low recurring revenue).

## What to Steal
- Their career blog with hundreds of "How to write a [job title] resume" articles drives enormous organic SEO traffic — CareerVivid should build programmatic landing pages for specific job titles powered by real Gemini-generated resume examples
