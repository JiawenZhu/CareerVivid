# Zety — Competitor Profile

> **Threat Level: 🟢 LOW**
> **Category fit:** Professional Services Access
> **XPRIZE risk:** 40M+/year visitors, $5.99/wk — massive SEO moat but zero AI-native depth

---

## Overview
Zety is one of the highest-traffic resume sites on the internet with 40M+ annual visitors, powered almost entirely by SEO content marketing. Their $5.99/week pricing is low-commitment and targets users who need a resume quickly. They have AI content suggestions (GPT) but the product is fundamentally a document creator with excellent templates and even better Google rankings.

## Key Capabilities
| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Resume Templates | 18+ professionally designed templates |
| AI Resume Content | GPT-based section suggestions |
| Cover Letter Builder | Template + suggestions |
| ATS Check | Basic keyword check |
| Career Advice Blog | 2,000+ articles ranking for career keywords |

## Business Model & Revenue
| Tier | Price | Features |
|---|---|---|
| Free (limited) | $0 | Preview only |
| Weekly | $5.99/wk | Download, edit, full access |
| Monthly | $23.70/mo | Same |

**Revenue estimate:** 40M visitors/yr × ~1% conversion to paid × $5.99/wk avg = enormous absolute revenue despite low per-user ARPU. Estimated $5M+/year revenue from scale alone. However, most users pay for 1–2 weeks then cancel.

## Tech Stack
- **AI:** GPT suggestions — **NOT Gemini**, minimal AI depth
- **Core:** Their moat is SEO content, not technology
- **No job tracker, no Chrome extension, no application management**

## Weaknesses vs CareerVivid
- No Gemini or Google Cloud
- No job application workflow
- No voice interview coaching
- No autonomous apply
- No CLI, no MCP server, no B2B portal
- Weekly subscription model = extremely high churn
- SEO moat doesn't translate to user loyalty or repeat purchases
- AI is cosmetic — their ranking comes from content writing, not AI products

## XPRIZE Assessment
Near-zero XPRIZE threat. Zety is an SEO-driven document mill with weekly subscriptions. Their "AI features" are checkbox items on GPT-generated content suggestions. They cannot score well on AI-Native Operations or demonstrate AI governing business decisions. Their revenue documentation would show high gross numbers but extremely high churn, which XPRIZE judges will see through.

## What to Steal
- Their SEO content strategy is unmatched — 2,000+ career articles rank page 1 for nearly every resume-related query. CareerVivid's blog content strategy should target "how to get a job at [Company]" keywords that Zety doesn't focus on
- The $5.99/week low-commitment pricing removes purchase anxiety — CareerVivid could offer a "7-day trial for $1" to convert fence-sitters
