# Champaign Public Outreach Plan

Last updated: 2026-06-02

## Scope

This outreach plan uses public business contacts only: official organization emails, official contact forms, public office phone numbers, and public directory listings that should be verified before larger sends. Do not use scraped personal emails, guessed employee patterns, private user data, or CareerVivid account emails for cold outreach.

Primary tracker:

- `docs/agency-partnerships/champaign-public-outreach-targets.csv`

## Best First Wave

Start with the `P0` rows in the CSV. They are the highest-fit local targets because they either place candidates directly or can introduce CareerVivid into a workforce-readiness channel.

Recommended first wave:

1. Local staffing and recruiting offices: Express, Specialized Recruiting Group, Spherion, Adecco, Manpower, Advocate Health Staffing.
2. Workforce and employer networks: Champaign County Chamber Talent Development, Champaign County EDC.
3. School and career services teams: Parkland Career Services, UIUC Career Center, Grainger Engineering Career Services, LAS Career Services.

Do not start with the broader startup/community list unless the staffing and career-services wave is already running. Those contacts are useful for referrals, events, and founder visibility, but less direct for early paid pilots.

## Recommended Offer

Lead with a zero-integration pilot:

> CareerVivid gives your branch or career-services team a prep link for 10 candidates. Candidates improve their resume, run AI review/interview prep, and share a recruiter-ready report only when they consent. Your team gets progress metrics without changing your ATS or workflow.

Keep the first offer simple:

- 10 candidate seats
- 14-day pilot
- no ATS integration
- candidate-controlled resume sharing
- branch dashboard and readiness reports

## Tracking Workflow

Use the `status` column in the CSV:

- `Not contacted`
- `Contacted`
- `Follow-up 1`
- `Follow-up 2`
- `Replied`
- `Demo scheduled`
- `Pilot agreed`
- `Closed - no fit`
- `Do not contact`

For each contacted organization, add a note with:

- date contacted
- channel used
- person or department reached if public
- offer used
- response summary
- next follow-up date

## Follow-Up Sequence

Day 1: Initial pilot offer.

Day 4: Add one concrete use case.

> Quick follow-up: the best early use case is candidates who are almost ready but need their resume cleaned up before recruiter or employer review. CareerVivid gives them a guided prep link and gives your team a readiness signal.

Day 8: Close the loop.

> I’ll close the loop here. If candidate resume prep becomes useful for your branch/program, I’d be happy to set up a small no-cost pilot.

Stop after the second follow-up unless they reply or explicitly invite more information.

## Compliance And Deliverability

- Use a dedicated outreach inbox or alias if volume increases.
- Configure SPF, DKIM, and DMARC before any scaled outreach.
- Keep initial volume low: 10-15 highly targeted public contacts per week.
- Use real organization names and specific context from their public site.
- Include a clear opt-out sentence for repeated outreach.
- Never contact private job seekers from CareerVivid product data for cold outreach.
- Verify directory-sourced emails by official site or phone before batch sending.
